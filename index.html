Strategic Creative Brief

By Walter Gangi

1. Strategic Creative Brief

  • Product Description:
    Bronze Bites are daily gummies that provide a natural, gradual, and long-lasting tan without the need for sun exposure. Formulated with natural plant-based pigments such as beta-carotene, lycopene, and astaxanthin, they deliver a healthy glow from within while protecting the skin from the harmful effects of UV rays.
  • Product Strengths:
    - Sunless Tanning: Ideal for those who want a golden tone without the risks of sunburn or premature aging.
    - Natural and safe ingredients: Plant-based pigments scientifically recognized for their antioxidant and pigmentation benefits.
    - Easy daily intake: Fun and convenient gummy format.
    - Gradual and lasting effect: Visible results in just a few days, perfect for maintaining a summer look all year round.
    - Aesthetic innovation: Positioned at the intersection of beauty, wellness, and convenience.
  • Target Audience:
    - Age: 20 to 35 years old.
    - Gender: Primarily women, but also open to men who care about their appearance.
    - Interests: Beauty, skincare, healthy living, natural products, effortless aesthetics, fashion, TikTok/IG trends.
    - Digital Behavior: Active social media content consumers (especially reels), familiar with viral trends, wellness, and self-care.
  • Strategic Rationale:
    The three proposed concepts address the consumer’s main pain point: the desire for an ideal tan without the negative effects of sun exposure or the effort of sunbathing.

2. Concept Development

Concept 1: NUCLEAR WHITE MAN

  • Style: Absurd sketch, classic humor (exaggerated yet relatable).
  • Visual Hook: Extreme contrast between pale and tanned skin.
  • Estimated Duration: 25 seconds.
  • Script:

    - [0:00 – 0:03]: A group of friends at a pool. All of them are tanned. One guy takes off his shirt...

    Someone shouts:

    "Oh my God!"

    (We see the "nuclear white" friend, his skin reflects light, the sun puts on sunglasses, and his friends burn like Sarah Connor in Terminator.)

    - [0:04 – 0:07]: Cut to the guy’s face as he eats a Bronze Bites gummy.

    Over-the-top transformation music (superhero style).

    - [0:08 – 0:15]: Flash-forward to…The same friend is now tanned. Everyone is shocked.

    One touches his arm.

    "Hey dude, what did you eat?"

    - [0:16 – 0:22]: Shot of the gummies.

    Voice-over (with a cocky tone):
    "Bronze Bites. Gummies with natural pigments for a lasting tan-without roasting under the sun."

    - [0:23 – 0:25]: Logo + slogan:

    "Don’t let your tan be a drama. Bronze Bites."

Concept 2: SOLAR DRAMA

  • Style: Telenovela parody / over-the-top drama.
  • Visual Hook: Fake soap opera scene, overly acted, with a badly sunburned back.
  • Estimated Duration: 30 seconds
  • Script:

    - [0:00 – 0:03]: Shot of a woman with a sunburned back, staring at the sun.

    Dramatic voice-over:

    "Why did you do this to me?!"

    - [0:04 – 0:08]: Cut to the woman throwing a bottle of coconut oil tanning lotion to the ground, crying.

    Text on screen: "It didn’t have to end like this…"

    - [0:09 – 0:15]: Another woman appears in a spa robe. Perfect tan.

    She says:

    "Fortunately I don't have that problem… I ate Bronze Bites!"

    - [0:16 – 0:25]: Telenovela-style transition to infomercial:

    Ironic voice-over:
    "With beta-carotene, lycopene, and astaxanthin. Get a tan without UV rays, without burning… and without the drama."

    - [0:26 – 0:30]: Logo appears, gummies glowing with a golden shine.

    Final line:

    "Don’t let your tan be a drama. Bronze Bites."

Concept 3: FROM PALE TO CARIBBEAN DEITY

  • Style: Spoof of shampoo or fitness commercial, exaggerated.
  • Visual Hook: Ridiculous “before and after” comparison.
  • Estimated Duration: 20 seconds.
  • Script:

    - [0:00 – 0:03]: Black and white shot. A very pale guy working in an office, looking exhausted and sad.

    Documentary-style text:

    "85% of men have a pale tan because they don’t have time to go to the beach."

    - [0:04 – 0:06]: Same guy eats a gummy, breaks into a big smile, and everything starts turning to color.

    Epic music begins

    - [0:07 – 0:15]: Fast transition: the guy is now at the gym, surrounded by people, everyone staring at him.

    One girl says:

    "I'm sure this guy goes to the beach a lot."

    - [0:16 – 0:20]: Final shot of the gummies.

    Voice-over:
    "Bronze Bites. From office pale to Caribbean gold… without UV rays."

3. Title & Thumbnail

Concept 1: NUCLEAR WHITE

  • Taps into viral humor and extreme visual contrast (very pale skin vs. tanned skin) to create relatability and laughs. The exaggerated sketch-style format is reminiscent of content that already performs well on social media, increasing the potential for virality and brand recall.

Concept 2: SOLAR DRAMA

  • Plays on the over-the-top drama of soap operas-an emotional language well-known and beloved in Latin America-making it perfect to highlight the absurdity of using outdated methods like oils and direct sun exposure. The parody grabs attention and creates emotional contrast.

Concept 3: FROM PALE TO CARIBBEAN DEITY

  • Uses the classic "before and after" format with a fitness or shampoo commercial aesthetic. The aspirational and visually transformational approach makes the result desirable, reinforcing the idea that anyone can achieve their dream tan effortlessly.

Why it will work

  • Relatable vanity appeal: Everyone wants to look good effortlessly. Bronze Bites delivers that result with humor, authenticity, and simplicity.
  • Highly shareable content: The absurd, parodic, and aspirational styles are designed to work as viral micro-sketches or reels.
  • Solution-focused, not fear-based: Instead of warning about UV rays, it offers a fresh and fun alternative.
  • Format versatility: Each concept can be adapted to multiple platforms (IG, TikTok, YouTube Shorts).

Final slogan

  • "Don’t let your tan be a drama. Bronze Bites."

Communication objective

  • To memorably and entertainingly show that Bronze Bites is the modern, safe, and effective alternative to achieve an enviable tan-without sun-related risks. The goal is to generate awareness, engagement, and immediate purchase desire.
